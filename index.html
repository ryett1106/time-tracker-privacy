Your data stays on your phone. Period.

We don't collect anything, track anything, or send anything anywhere. Time Tracker Pro is built with privacy first - all your time entries, settings, and information stay completely private on your device.

1. Introduction

Time Tracker Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your information.

Time Tracker Pro is designed with privacy as the foundation. We believe your work hours and time tracking data are personal and should remain completely private.

2. Information Collection

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use Time Tracker Pro, you may enter the following information:

  • Your Name: Used to personalize your experience within the app
  • Job Title: Displayed in the app interface
  • Time Entries: Clock in/out times, job/project names, and lunch break records
  • Customization Preferences: Theme choices, background images, notification settings

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

Time Tracker Pro does NOT collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers. We do not use:

  • Analytics services (no usage tracking)
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Third-party tracking services
  • Cloud storage or backup services
  • Location services
  • Social media integrations

3. How We Store Your Data

All data is stored locally on your device only.

Time Tracker Pro uses your device's secure local storage to save:

  • Your time entries and work history
  • User preferences and settings
  • Custom background images (if uploaded)
  • Theme and customization choices

Important: Your data never leaves your device. We have no access to your information because it's never transmitted to us or anyone else.

4. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device:

  • You have complete control over your information
  • Your data is as secure as your device security (passcode, biometric authentication)
  • No one can access your data remotely (including us)
  • Your information cannot be compromised by server breaches or hacks

We recommend: Using your device's built-in security features (Face ID, Touch ID, or passcode) to protect your Time Tracker Pro data.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with anyone because we never have access to it.

Time Tracker Pro does not:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your information with advertisers
  • Provide your data to analytics companies
  • Transmit your information to external servers
  • Allow any third-party access to your time entries

6. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

6.1 Access Your Data

All your time entries are visible within the app at any time. You can view your complete work history in the "Recent Entries" section.

6.2 Export Your Data

Use the "Export Timesheet (CSV)" button to download all your time entries as a CSV file. This file is generated locally on your device and can be saved, emailed, or shared as you choose.

6.3 Delete Your Data

You can delete individual time entries within the app, or completely remove all data by uninstalling the app. Once uninstalled, all your Time Tracker Pro data is permanently deleted from your device.

6.4 Control Settings

You control all app permissions including notifications, sound, and vibration through the Settings menu.

7. Permissions

Time Tracker Pro may request the following device permissions:

7.1 Notifications (Optional)

Used only to alert you when lunch breaks end. You can enable or disable this at any time in Settings. No notification content is ever sent to external servers.

7.2 Vibration (Optional)

Used for haptic feedback to enhance the user experience. Can be disabled in Settings.

7.3 Storage Access

Used only to save your time entries and settings locally on your device. Required for the app to function.

7.4 Photo Library (Optional)

Only accessed if you choose to upload a custom background image. Images are stored locally and never uploaded anywhere.

We do NOT request or use: Location, camera, microphone, contacts, calendar, or any other sensitive permissions.

8. Third-Party Services

Time Tracker Pro does NOT use any third-party services.

There are no:

  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage services
  • Payment processors (app is one-time purchase through App Store/Play Store only)

9. Children's Privacy

Time Tracker Pro is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information to the app, please contact us and we will provide guidance on removing the app and its data.

10. Data Retention

Your data is retained locally on your device for as long as:

  • You keep the app installed, and/or
  • You choose not to delete individual entries

When you uninstall Time Tracker Pro, all data is permanently deleted from your device. We cannot recover this data because we never had access to it.

11. International Users

Time Tracker Pro can be used anywhere in the world. Since all data stays on your device, there are no international data transfer concerns. Your data remains within your physical possession at all times.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • For significant changes, we will display a notification in the app
  • The updated policy will be available at this URL

Continued use of Time Tracker Pro after changes indicates acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

13. Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). However, since Time Tracker Pro does not collect, store, or share any personal information, these rights are inherently protected:

  • Right to Know: All your data is visible in the app
  • Right to Delete: Uninstall the app to delete all data
  • Right to Opt-Out: There is nothing to opt out of - we don't collect data
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: All users have the same experience

14. European Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since Time Tracker Pro operates entirely on-device:

  • We are not a data controller or processor
  • No personal data is transferred outside the EEA
  • You have complete control as you are the sole custodian of your data
  • No consent is required for data collection because no collection occurs
